Depression

Depression is a mood disorder that causes a persistent feeling of sadness and loss of interest. It affects how you feel, think, and behave and can lead to a variety of emotional and physical problems.

Common symptoms of depression include feelings of hopelessness, changes in appetite and sleep patterns, loss of energy, difficulty concentrating, and thoughts of self-harm or suicide.

Treatment for depression may include therapy, medication, and lifestyle changes. It's important to reach out for support if you or someone you know is experiencing symptoms of depression.
