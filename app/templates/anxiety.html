Anxiety

Anxiety is a feeling of unease, such as worry or fear, that can be mild or severe. It is a normal and often healthy emotion. However, when anxiety becomes excessive or persistent, it can interfere with daily life and well-being.

Common symptoms of anxiety include restlessness, irritability, difficulty concentrating, and trouble sleeping. It can also manifest physically, with symptoms such as rapid heartbeat, sweating, and shortness of breath.

There are various treatment options available for anxiety, including therapy, medication, and self-help techniques. It's important to seek help if anxiety is significantly impacting your life.
